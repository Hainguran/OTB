OTB Sport is not only a sports retailer that offers the latest sports gear, apparel, footwear and fashion, they also offer team wear, functional sports equipment, trophies and medals to a broad range of customers in Namibia. they are headquartered in Windhoek with retail outlets around the country. they employ sales representatives who travel around the country vesting clubs, schools, sports federations and companies. The OTB team sport department focuses on our club, school and institutional customers with a broad offering of team kits, trophies, medals, hardware and accessories. And they also offer printing, embroidery, laser printing and engraving services on apparel, kits, trophies, medals and gifts.
